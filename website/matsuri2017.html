Japan Festival at University of Washington

May 6th 2017 5–9PM

What is Matsuri? Matsuri is JSA’s most popular annual event, and the largest celebration of Japanese culture on campus. Unlike our other (fantastic) events throughout the year, Matsuri’s appeal extends beyond the UW community, inviting everyone of any age to come together and enjoy the festivities throughout the day. This year’s theme is ‘Edo Jidai’, or The Edo Period. We'll be transforming the HUB Ballroom into a traditional Japanese festival, complete with food stands, games, and performances – come hungry!

What is JSA UW? JSA UW, or the Japanese Student Association at University of Washington, is a student organization dedicated to celebrating and proliferating both Japanese and American culture. We offer events throughout the year designed to inform students about various traditions and practices in Japan and America in a fun, social environment.

Main Performer

Ayumu Yamashita

Ayumu Yamashita is a musician and filmmaker who has found a substantial following on YouTube, performing original songs and covers of popular favorites. He has a wonderful voice and a warm personality, and we can’t wait to see him own the stage at Matsuri!
